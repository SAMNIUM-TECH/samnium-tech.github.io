Samnium Tech is born!#

This is the first blog post on SAMNIUM TECH.

SAMNIUM TECH was founded with the mission of bringing to market the expertise accumulated through over a decade of experience in both hardware and software projects, particularly within the embedded field. Our goal extends beyond designing and selling innovative products; we are committed to providing specialized consulting services to assist companies in need.

Our team excels in offering expert consulting in embedded Linux, as well as real-time operating systems such as FreeRTOS, NuttX, ZephyrOS, and others. These skills find application in diverse sectors such as IoT, Automotive, Industrial, Medical, and Military.
Over time, we have developed advanced proficiency in connected systems, enabling us to construct operating systems from the ground up, ensuring secure remote updates (from the device to the finalized cloud service).

To complete our repertoire, we possess significant expertise in IT security. We provide specialized security audits for devices and protocols, contributing to the overall safety and integrity of our clients’ systems.
